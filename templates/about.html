Work Experience

I am a ski industry profesional with varied experience in the manufacturing, retail, and resort sectors. I have spent time on the factory lines for both soft-goods and hard-goods. I have also started my own mock manufacturing business advertising sewn goods. During my time working with soft-goods, specifically outdoor techincal apparel, I taught myself the basics of sewing. Well, with a little help from my mother and the designer at work... I've started to develop quite the love-hate relationship with manufacturing. On one hand, a well run factory line is just about the most mind-numbing thing I've ever experienced. But on the other hand I love problem solving, and due to the constant nature of manufacturing, there's always a problem to solve.

Education

I received a Bachelor's of Science from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst in 2018, with a dual concentration in Applied Mathematics & Statistics. While in school, I was exposed to the Python programming language, and have kept working with Python since graduation. These days, I mostly use the web development framework Django to build websites.
